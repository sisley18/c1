👩‍🏫 About the Author
CC

Cecilia Chiappero

English Language Educator & Researcher | Universidad Nacional de Córdoba

Cecilia Chiappero is an English language educator and researcher affiliated with the Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, Argentina. Her academic work focuses on innovative teaching methodologies and technology-enhanced language learning.

She has published research on the use of Moodle to promote listening comprehension strategies in the English Language classroom. Her paper "El uso de Moodle para fomentar el uso de estrategias de comprensión auditiva en el aula de Lengua Inglesa I" (2011) demonstrates her expertise in integrating digital tools into effective language instruction.

This C1 Advanced English Course represents her continued dedication to creating comprehensive, accessible, and engaging materials for advanced learners seeking to refine their English proficiency to near-native levels.
